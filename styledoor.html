Style Door

OYSTO Style door frame is the new alternative for the aging solid wooden door with similar sound insulation abilities at a relatively better price point. It can be used as a swing as well as a sliding door. It consists of a 75x44 mm profile to be used as a four sided frame with a layer of glass within the frame.

Style Door 65x35

OYSTO Style Door 65x35 modular aluminum framed door system is designed to complement modern glass partition solutions. It offers excellent strength, durability, and a sleek appearance suitable for contemporary office interiors. The system is engineered to provide smooth operation and a clean, minimal design that enhances the overall workspace environment. It consists of a 65x35 mm aluminum profile section that supports toughened glass panels and ensures structural stability. The clip-on installation system allows quick and efficient door fitting, making it ideal for office cabins, meeting rooms, and commercial spaces.
