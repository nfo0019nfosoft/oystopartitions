About OYSTO

OYSTO is among the pioneering suppliers of architectural interior products and solutions designed for modern commercial and office environments. Our systems are developed to create beautiful, functional, and well-organized interior spaces that enhance productivity and visual appeal. We assist architects, designers, and project consultants by offering a wide range of customized solutions tailored to the requirements of each workspace and project.

Our Modular Glass Partition Systems are designed to provide flexible and efficient space management for offices, hospitals, and commercial establishments. These partitions allow easy installation, modification, and rearrangement whenever workspace requirements change. In addition to partition systems, OYSTO also provides aluminum skirting, interior accessories, and door hardware solutions that integrate seamlessly with modern interior designs to deliver a refined and professional finish.

APPLICATIONS

Modular Glass Partition Systems, Aluminum Skirting and other interior accessories and door hardware offered by OYSTO are widely used for optimization and compartmentalization of work-space across various professional fields such as Corporate Offices, Bank, Research Laboratories, Educational Universities, Building Entrance Systems, Industrial Manufacturing Facilities, Conference/Convention Halls, Hospitals and Clinics.

Besides these professional applications our products have also been applied in modern/smart home designs as home owners today are keen on non-conventional styles of home interior design.

With a focus on durability, precision engineering, and aesthetic appeal, OYSTO solutions are designed to integrate seamlessly with a wide range of architectural styles while providing long-term performance and reliability in both commercial and residential environments.
