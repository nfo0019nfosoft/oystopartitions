Neon Series - 100

OYSTO Neon Series 100 modular aluminum single glaze partition system offers the best rigidity and strength due to its heavy profile. It is also aesthetically pleasing due to the glass to profile ratio. It consists of a section dimension of 25x100 mm and is a clip on system for easy glass installation.

FINISHES Silver / Black / Other Anodised

Neon Double Series (Dual Track)

OYSTO Wide Double Glaze (Dual Track) modular aluminum single glaze partition system offers high strength and rigidity. However its main purpose is to provide the best sound insulation by adding an extra layer of glass and also an air gap between the two glass layers. It consists of a section dimension of 25x100 mm dual track and is a clip on system for easier glass installation.

PROFILE 25 x 100 MM(Dual Track)
FINISHES Silver / Black / Other Anodised
DOOR FRAME Can be used with cover or rebate profile
GLASS THICKNESS 8, 10 and 12 MM
